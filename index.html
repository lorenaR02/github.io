Puberty and Menstruation
Puberty is a period when our bodies experience higher levels of hormonal changes. Due to those higher levels of hormones young people also experience rapid growth, development, and emotional changes.
Talk to Someone
Going through puberty can be a very confusing and scary time for young people to go through. For some people that don't have a support group or anyone to talk to about certain topics usually rely on the internet for information which sometimes aren't always reliable and lead people to be misinformed.
Sexual Health
Sexual health is a state of physical, mental and social well-being in relation to sexuality. It requires a positive and respectful approach to sexuality and sexual relationships, as well as the possibility of having pleasurable and safe sexual experiences, free of coercion, discrimination and violence.
