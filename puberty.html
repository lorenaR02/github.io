Puberty

What is Puberty?

Puberty happens when kids are around 8-13 years old. It might be confusing time because your body starts to change and it might be some getting use to your developing body. Some signs that you might notice happening to you might be:

  • Breast start to grow
  • Hair growth on arms, armpits, legs, and pubic area
  • Increasing of sweat and odor
  • Pimples
  • Increase of height or weight
  • Growing pains

    • Puberty Blockers

After talking to your gardian about this you can talk to your doctor about taking treatment to get treatment. This medicine blocks two harmones that change our body called testosterone and estrogen.

  • A flexible rod called histrelin acetate that goes under the skin of the arm and lasts for 1 year.
  • A shot called leuprolide acetate, which works for 1, 3, or 4 months at a time.
